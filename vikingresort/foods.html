Local Foods

Viking Bread

Try some Viking breads for any meal. This hearty and heavy breads are perfect for serving with soups and stews. It is also a good choice to warm from the oven and drizzled with honey! Milk are also available if you like.

Salmon

Salmon has been a popular seafood in modern lifes, especially the salmon sashimi. but have you tried to eat salmon in a Viking way? Come and try our traditional 'Salmon Stew', and you will find this is a dish worth fighting for! Not suit for you? no worries! we also have the recipe that you familiar, try our sandwich with salmon steak in it, and adventure like a warrior!

Viking Lamb

Meats are important recipes for Viking warriors. Our Viking Lamb Kebabs are kid-friendly, and has tender , flavourful bites of lamb shoulder, fresh tart apples, crunchy grilled vegetables and tender mushrooms and onions. Does this make you think of the legend of dragon? Come and have a try!
