SmartQ

SmartQ is a digital questionnaire system designed for clinicians and medical students in the course Digital Health Software Project. The main purpose is to develop an online questionnaire platform on mobile phone that allows clinicians to collect and document patients' health information more easily, and also provides an practical opportunity for UQ medical students about the medical system in Australia.
