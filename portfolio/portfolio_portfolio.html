PORTFOLIO ABOUT ME

This Portfolio

The style of portfolio reflects author's personality, in order to present an interesting and happy me, I decided to make it a hand-drawn style. Consider the formative and the impression of my work for the audience, hand-drawn elements are only designed as decorations and supportive things, and the main content are more formal and consist with the theme of the project. So I drew some decorations and illustration images on my iPad using a chalk-like brush although I'm not good at it (seems that the clouds are the most successful ones).

The main color theme are designed as dark blue, because it can provides a sense of steady, rational, and technology. It also has a feeling of high-ended as I utilized the font Futura, which is commonly used in the design related with fashion and future. Another font LittlePat was used on the navigation bar to fit the hand-drawn elements, and because it is more regular as a handwritten font, it could perfectly fit the theme of this portfolio as some supplementary elements. Unfortunately this font is not a complete version and it lacks numbers and most of the symbol characters.

As the "Survive" project is the main part of this portfolio, the page will have a large amount of content and it is important to present the design process and reflections. With the thought of visualizing the process and referencing one of the paper that I reviewed, I decided to use parallax scrolling to present animations and visual effects that could illustrate the narrative process in a better way. The position transfer in scrolling are used in inspiration bubbles and data accessing to present a bubbling effect and the flow of data. While the main design style changes used fixed background to present a effect that is commonly used in some trailer videos and fashion websites. So as the audience scroll down the webpage, they could have an intuitive and interesting view of of the design process. And the dynamic box shadow can provide a sense of stereoscopic of portpolio gallery. Besides, this has also been used at the clouds on the homepage to make them "floating in the sky".
